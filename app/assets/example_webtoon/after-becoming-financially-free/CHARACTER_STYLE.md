# Character Style Guide - After Becoming Financially Free

## === MALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 7.5-8 heads tall (Modern athletic build).
Build Range: Lean to muscular. Defined neck muscles and broad shoulders but less exaggerated than fantasy warriors.
Structure: Realistic modern anatomy. Clothes fit tightly to show musculature.
Hands/Feet: Large, somewhat blocky hands. Fingers are thick and masculine.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Long, sharp oval faces. Pointed chin.
Eyes: Sharp, narrower almonds. Often hidden by bangs or shadowed for mystery/mood.
  - Construction: Angular lines.
  - Detail Level: Simplified irises compared to female characters.
  - Lashes/Brows: Thick straight eyebrows.
Nose: Sharp triangular wedge in profile. Vertical line in front view.
Mouth: Simple line mouth, rarely lip-defined unless close-up.
Ears: Standardized, simplified interior lines.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: Moderate natural volume.
Texture System: Spiky or messy layers.
Strand Method: Chunky distinct locks.
Line Style: Sharp, decisive strokes.
Highlight Method: "Zig-zag" or "M" shaped glossy white band on the curve of the head.
Physics: Relatively static, heavy hair feel.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Young adults (20s). Smooth skin.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Deadpan, serious, slight smirk.
Exaggerated Range: Sweat drops, shadow over eyes for intensity/anger.
Eye Changes: Narrowing.
Mouth Changes: Minimal.
Special Effects: Gloom lines, steam (if angry/hot).

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Thick, consistent black contour.
Details: Minimal internal lines on face.
Hair Lines: Sharp/spiky.
Material Variation: Uniform line weight mostly.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Tan/Warm Beige.
Shading Method: Cell shading for structure + soft airbrush for blushing/depth.
Shadow Type: Grey/Brown hard shadows.
Highlight Placement: Shine on nose bridge, lower lip.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
The "Hidden Eyes" protagonist look. Sharp, confident modern style.

## === FEMALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 6.5-7 heads tall (Curvy, idealized).
Build Range: Hourglass figures emphasized. Slender limbs but full bust/hips.
Structure: Soft shoulders, defined waist. Perspective often emphasizes curves.
Hands/Feet: Small, delicate, tapered fingers.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Soft V-shape/Heart shape. Rounded cheeks.
Eyes: Large, rounder almonds.
  - Construction: Thick upper lash line (eyeliner look).
  - Detail Level: Moderate. Iris has gradient and 1-2 sharp white highlights.
  - Lashes/Brows: Distinct individual lashes at outer corners. Thin arched brows.
Nose: Tiny dot or small wedge. Very simplified.
Mouth: Plump, glossy lips. Often drawn with a "pouty" gap.
Ears: Simplified.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: Smooth, manageable volume. Sleek looks.
Texture System: Shiny, conditioned look.
Strand Method: Large smooth masses with few internal split lines.
Line Style: Smooth curves.
Highlight Method: Sharp white glossy band (Angel ring).
Physics: Bouncy, follows movement lines.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Young adult (20s). Flawless skin.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Pouting, side-eye.
Exaggerated Range: "Chibi-fied" shock (blank white eyes, round mouth), sweat drops, angry veins.
Eye Changes: Pupils shrink for shock.
Mouth Changes: O-shape for surprise.
Special Effects: Heavy blush (hatched red lines + pink gradient), hearts, sparkles.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Clean black lines, slightly thinner than male.
Details: Minimal face lines.
Hair Lines: Smooth.
Material Variation: Clothing folds are sharp/geometric.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Fair/Pinkish.
Shading Method: Soft airbrush on boobs/thighs/cheeks to emphasize roundness. Cell shade on clothes.
Shadow Type: Warm pink shadows on skin.
Highlight Placement: Heavy gloss on lips (white dot), nose tip, and chest.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
Glossy/shiny skin texture and lips ("Moist" look). Chibi reaction faces for comedy.

---

## === UNIVERSAL STYLE NOTES ===

GENDER DIMORPHISM LEVEL: High. Males are sharp/hard; Females are soft/round/glossy.

CONSISTENCY RULES:
- Glossy highlight band on hair.
- Red hatched blush lines for embarrassment/arousal.
- Clean vector-like linework.

VARIATION ALLOWANCES:
- Comedy scenes allow significant deformation (round eyes, simplified bodies).

AGE RANGE: Adults only (Office/College setting).

EXPRESSION SPECTRUM: Modern Rom-Com (Oscillates between sexy/moody and goofy/comedy).

OVERALL AESTHETIC: Modern Webtoon / Manhwa (Adult/Seinen skew).
