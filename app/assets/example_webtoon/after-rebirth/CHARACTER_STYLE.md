# Character Style Guide - After Rebirth

## === MALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 8-9 heads tall (Heroic/Action proportions). Ethereal elongation.
Build Range: Slim but strong (Agile Hunter build). Long limbs, broad shoulders but not bulky.
Structure: Dynamic S-curves in posture. V-taper torsos.
Hands/Feet: Stylized, somewhat large hands for casting poses. Fingers are long and angular.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Sharp diamond/wedge shape. Very pointed chin.
Eyes: Intense, glowing, often simplified to glowing shapes without pupils in power modes.
  - Construction: Sharp angles, "hunter eyes".
  - Detail Level: High contrast. Glowing irises (Yellow/Blue/Purple).
  - Lashes/Brows: Simple sharp eyebrows.
Nose: Sharp vertical line or small wedge shadow.
Mouth: Minimalist line.
Ears: Standard.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: "Anime protagonist" spikes. Gravity-defying.
Texture System: Clumped shadows.
Strand Method: Large jagged clumps.
Line Style: Sharp, energetic lines.
Highlight Method: Rim lighting (the hair often catches the color of the aura/magic).
Physics: Blowing in magical wind.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Young adult (20s). Ageless power look.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Stoic, determination.
Exaggerated Range: Gritting teeth (battle), glowing eyes flair.
Eye Changes: Eyes become pure light sources.
Mouth Changes: Shouting (simple shapes).
Special Effects: Auras, magical particles floating around face.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Thinner than western comics but crisp.
Details: Minimal internal face lines, focus on outfit details.
Hair Lines: Sharp.
Material Variation: Armor is drawn with cleaner/harder lines than cloth.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Pale/Fair (contrasts with dark clothes).
Shading Method: Dynamic Cell Shading (Hard edge shadows).
Shadow Type: Dramatic Cast Shadows (black/dark blue).
Highlight Placement: Rim lighting is KEY. Characters are often lit by their own magic.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
The "Solo Leveling" aesthetic: Glowing eyes, dark coats, system windows, purple/black auras.

## === FEMALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===
*(Note: Based on action genre standards as limited female samples were viewed, assuming consistent style)*

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 7-8 heads tall.
Build Range: Athletic, fit.
Structure: Defined waist, long legs for action.
Hands/Feet: Elegant but functional.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Soft V-shape.
Eyes: Large, expressive, often glowing if mage-class.
  - Construction: Sharp eyeliner.
  - Detail Level: High.
  - Lashes/Brows: Distinct.
Nose: Small point.
Mouth: Small.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: Flowing, decorative.
Texture System: Smooth.
Strand Method: Long flowing masses.
Highlight Method: Glossy bands + Rim light.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Young adult.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Concern, determination.
Exaggerated Range: Combat shouting.
Special Effects: Magic casting auras.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Clean, uniform.
Material Variation: Tight fitting armor/suits.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Fair.
Shading Method: Cell shading.
Highlight Placement: Glossy highlights on armor/latex suits.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
Action-oriented pose flow, magical effects integration.

---

## === UNIVERSAL STYLE NOTES ===

GENDER DIMORPHISM LEVEL: Moderate. Both genders are idealized action heroes.

CONSISTENCY RULES:
- **Glowing Eyes**: Key indicator of power/emotion.
- **Rim Lighting**: Characters are almost always rim-lit by magic colors (purple, blue, gold).
- **System Aesthetic**: UI elements and flat graphical effects mixed with painted characters.

VARIATION ALLOWANCES:
- Monsters are drawn with much heavier, rougher lines and textures than humans.

AGE RANGE: Young Action Heroes.

EXPRESSION SPECTRUM: Action/Shonen styling (Cool > Emotional).

OVERALL AESTHETIC: Modern Action Fantasy / Hunter Manhwa.
