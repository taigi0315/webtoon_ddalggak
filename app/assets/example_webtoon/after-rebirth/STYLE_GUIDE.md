## === WEBTOON VISUAL STYLE GUIDE ===

### CORE AESTHETIC
Premium historical fantasy Korean webtoon with rich painterly rendering and dramatic cinematic composition. Features highly detailed ornamental costume design, sophisticated color grading, and theatrical lighting. Balances realistic anatomy with idealized beauty standards. Strong emphasis on luxury, elegance, and emotional intensity through visual opulence.

### SHOT COMPOSITION

**Establishing shots:**
- Grand architectural spaces with detailed palace interiors
- Ornate environmental details (chandeliers, columns, decorative elements)
- Wide vertical panels emphasizing height and grandeur
- Atmospheric depth with layered architectural elements

**Medium shots:**
- Elegant character framing from waist up
- Emphasis on costume details and jewelry
- Sophisticated positioning with graceful poses
- Rich background context maintained

**Close-ups:**
- Highly detailed facial features with dramatic expressions
- Intense eye contact and emotional nuance
- Ornamental hair accessories and jewelry in frame
- Selective focus on emotional beats

**Dramatic shots:**
- Low-angle shots for power and authority
- High-contrast lighting for tension
- Silhouettes and backlighting for mystery
- Dynamic angles for emotional impact

### VISUAL BEATS RHYTHM

**Detail beats:**
- Extreme detail on jewelry, lace, embroidery
- Textural rendering of fabrics (silk, velvet, brocade)
- Close-ups of hands with rings and ornaments
- Intimate object focus (teacups, letters, accessories)

**Emotional beats:**
- Dramatic facial expressions with tears, blush, intensity
- Character interactions with physical proximity
- Reaction shots with high emotional readability
- Romantic tension through framing and lighting

**Power beats:**
- Authoritative poses and commanding presence
- Regal costumes and crown/tiara emphasis
- Architectural grandeur supporting character status
- Dramatic lighting emphasizing hierarchy

### LINE ART

**Line weight**: Variable weight with refined precision
- Medium to thick outlines for character contours
- Delicate thin lines for lace, embroidery, and fine details
- Consistent clean quality with painterly integration
- Lines often softened or dissolved into shading

**Line quality**: Semi-painterly digital with refined edges
- Clean but not overly graphic
- Lines blend into shading for soft transitions
- Precise architectural and ornamental linework
- Expressive facial feature definition

**Detail density**: 
- Extremely high detail on costumes and accessories
- Intricate patterns on fabrics and jewelry
- Detailed hair rendering with individual strands
- Rich environmental detail in backgrounds
- Strategic simplification for visual hierarchy

**Stylistic approach**:
- Characters: Refined outlines with painterly interior rendering
- Costumes: Extreme detail with pattern and texture emphasis
- Backgrounds: Architectural precision with atmospheric treatment
- Effects: Integrated into painterly rendering

### COLOR PALETTE

**Dominant colors**: 
- Rich jewel tones (deep burgundy, royal purple, emerald green)
- Luxurious golds and champagne highlights
- Pure whites and creams for contrast
- Deep shadows with warm undertones
- Saturated accent colors for emotional emphasis

**Color temperature**: 
- Warm bias with golden lighting for intimate scenes
- Cool shifts for dramatic or tense moments
- Candlelight warmth for historical atmosphere
- Temperature contrasts for depth and mood

**Saturation strategy**:
- High saturation on costumes and key elements
- Rich, vibrant colors for luxury feel
- Desaturated backgrounds for character focus
- Selective saturation for emotional emphasis
- Gradient saturation for atmospheric depth

**Color coding**:
- Gold/champagne = luxury, royalty, power
- Deep reds/burgundy = passion, drama, intensity
- Royal purple = nobility, mystery, elegance
- Cool blues = distance, melancholy, reflection
- Warm candlelight = intimacy, romance, vulnerability

**Contrast levels**:
- High contrast for dramatic scenes
- Moderate contrast for balanced readability
- Low contrast for soft romantic moments
- Strategic contrast for visual hierarchy

### LIGHTING & ATMOSPHERE

**Light direction**:
- Dramatic directional lighting (often from above or side)
- Candlelight and chandelier illumination
- Window light with strong directional quality
- Theatrical rim lighting for silhouettes
- Multiple light sources for rich complexity

**Mood lighting**:
- Warm candlelight for intimate historical atmosphere
- Cool moonlight for night scenes
- Golden palace lighting for grandeur
- Dramatic shadows for tension and mystery
- Soft diffused light for tender moments

**Shadow rendering**:
- Rich gradient shadows with warm undertones
- Deep shadows with colored fill (not pure black)
- Dramatic shadow shapes for mood
- Soft-edge shadows for gentle scenes
- Hard-edge shadows for dramatic contrast

**Highlight style**:
- Strong specular highlights on jewelry and metal
- Glossy highlights on silk and satin fabrics
- Luminous highlights on eyes (multiple catchlights)
- Rim lighting on hair creating halo effects
- Wet shine on lips and emotional tears

**Atmospheric effects**:
- Volumetric light rays through windows
- Candlelight glow and flame effects
- Sparkle particles for magical or emotional moments
- Soft focus blur for romantic atmosphere
- Dust particles in dramatic light beams

**Time of day indicators**:
- Warm golden = afternoon palace scenes
- Cool blue = night or moonlight
- Candlelight amber = evening interiors
- Neutral balanced = formal day scenes

**Formula**: `[Base Premium Historical Style] + [Specific Scene Details]`


### Style Consistency Tips

1. **Always include**: "Premium historical fantasy Korean webtoon style"
2. **Emphasize detail**: Costume, jewelry, and ornamental elements
3. **Rich colors**: Jewel tones, gold, deep saturated hues
4. **Dramatic lighting**: Candlelight, theatrical illumination
5. **Painterly quality**: Refined edges, gradient shading
6. **Emotional intensity**: Expressive features, dramatic moments
7. **Historical context**: Palace settings, period costumes
8. **Luxury aesthetic**: Opulence, elegance, refinement

### Adaptation for Different Scenes

**Romantic scenes**: Warm candlelight, soft focus, sparkles, intimate framing, blush effects
**Power scenes**: Grand architecture, low angles, dramatic lighting, regal costumes
**Tense scenes**: High contrast, cool tones, intense expressions, dramatic shadows
**Emotional peaks**: Tears, intense colors, dramatic effects, close-ups
