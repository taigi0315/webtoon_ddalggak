# Character Style Guide - For Your Perfect Endidng

## === MALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 7-7.5 heads tall (Youthful high school build).
Build Range: Slim, lanky. Not overly muscular.
Structure: Realistic teenage frame. Shoulders are distinct but not exaggeratedly wide.
Hands/Feet: Detailed but "soft" hands. Bony rather than muscular.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Soft oval/round faces. Gentle chin curve.
Eyes: Large, gentle almond shape. Heavy upper lid.
  - Construction: Soft curved lines rather than sharp angles.
  - Detail Level: High. Irises have a "galaxy/stardust" texture with soft gradients (purple/blue).
  - Lashes/Brows: Long, distinct lower lashes often utilized. Natural brows.
Nose: Small, soft definition. Often just a small shadow.
Mouth: Small, soft lips. Not heavily outlined.
Ears: Round, prominent in short hair styles.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: Fluffy, soft volume.
Texture System: Matte finish.
Strand Method: Fine, individual strands mixed with soft masses.
Line Style: Pencil-like, sketchy but clean.
Highlight Method: Diffuse soft light. No sharp "angel rings".
Physics: Gentle, calm movement.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Teenagers/Young Adults. Softness of youth emphasized.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Melancholy, gentle smiles, blank stares.
Exaggerated Range: Minimal. Emotions are internalized.
Eye Changes: Pupillary constriction for shock.
Mouth Changes: Subtle curvature.
Special Effects: Blushing (soft airbrush), gloom lines.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Soft, textured lines (pencil/charcoal brush feel). Dark grey rather than black.
Details: Very few face lines.
Hair Lines: Delicate.
Material Variation: Uniform softness.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Pale, translucent.
Shading Method: Painterly, soft blotting. A "grainy" or noise texture overlay is applied to the whole image.
Shadow Type: Violet/Blue cool shadows.
Highlight Placement: Soft, diffuse.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
The "Nostalgic Texture" (Noise/Paper grain overlay). Soft, dreamy, pencil-like aesthetic.

## === FEMALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 6-6.5 heads tall.
Build Range: Average, realistic teenage girl.
Structure: Soft curves, practical build (tracksuits, uniforms).
Hands/Feet: Small, delicate.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Round/Heart-shaped face. Soft jawline.
Eyes: Large, round.
  - Construction: Thick upper lash line.
  - Detail Level: Soft, dreamy.
  - Lashes/Brows: Natural, feathery lashes.
Nose: Button nose (small dot/curve).
Mouth: Small rosebud lips.
Ears: Small.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: Natural, manageable volume.
Texture System: Soft, fluffy.
Strand Method: Gentle curves.
Line Style: Sketchy/Soft.
Highlight Method: Soft diffuse sheen.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Teenager.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Worry, tenderness.
Exaggerated Range: Wide-eyed shock.
Special Effects: Soft rosy cheeks.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Textured, charcoal-style lines.
Details: Minimal.
Hair Lines: Soft.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Pale/Fair.
Shading Method: Soft painterly style.
Shadow Type: Cool tones.
Highlight Placement: Minimal, matte skin finish.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
Softness. Every edge is slightly blurred or textured. No harsh digital cuts.

---

## === UNIVERSAL STYLE NOTES ===

GENDER DIMORPHISM LEVEL: Low-Moderate. Both genders share a "soft/beautiful" aesthetic.

CONSISTENCY RULES:
- **Texture Overlay**: The noise/grain texture is essential.
- **Soft Lines**: No hard vector lines.
- **Cool Palette**: Tends towards blues/purples/greys.

VARIATION ALLOWANCES:
- Children are drawn with "Storybook" proportions (very round).

AGE RANGE: Adolescence.

EXPRESSION SPECTRUM: Melancholic School Life / Soft Romance.

OVERALL AESTHETIC: Modern Manhwa with Retro/Analogue Texture.
