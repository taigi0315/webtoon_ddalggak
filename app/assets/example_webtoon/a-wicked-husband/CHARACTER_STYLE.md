# Character Style Guide - A Wicked Husband

## === MALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 8-9 heads tall (tall, elegant, powerful build)
Build Range: Lean muscular to broad athletic. Broad shoulders are emphasized.
Structure: V-tapered torsos, defined clavicles and neck muscles. Shoulders are significantly wider than hips.
Hands/Feet: Large, realist hands with defined knuckles, tendons, and fingernails. Veins often visible in tense moments.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Sharp, angular jawlines ending in a defined chin. Faces are generally longer/oval.
Eyes: Narrower than female eyes but still large and expressive. Intense gaze.
  - Construction: Sharp corners, often semi-lidded or intense stare. High upper eyelid crease.
  - Detail Level: Highly detailed irises with multiple light reflections. Pupils often sharp.
  - Lashes/Brows: Distinct, thick eyebrows (often dark/black). Eyelashes present but less voluminous than female.
Nose: High, straight bridge. Defined nostrils in close-ups, often just a sharp line/shadow in medium shots.
Mouth: Wide mouth, distinctly drawn lips (both upper and lower often defined, unlike some anime styles).
Ears: Realistic rendering, often detailed with inner cartilage when visible.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: Moderate volume, follows skull shape but with aesthetic flow.
Texture System: Silky, distinct strands.
Strand Method: Defined individual clumps with stray hairs for realism.
Line Style: Finest linework, often tapering to nothing.
Highlight Method: "Angel ring" or soft glossy band highlight, plus individual strand highlights.
Physics: Flows dramatically with wind/movement.

AGE REPRESENTATION: Young adults (20s-30s) are primary. Smooth skin but with manly definition.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Micro-expressions in eyebrows and mouth corners.
Exaggerated Range: Intense insanity/anger (wide eyes, bared teeth, veins). No chibi deformation.
Eye Changes: Squinting for suspicion, widening for shock/obsessiveness.
Mouth Changes: Smirks, snarls (showing detailed teeth).
Special Effects: Gloomy vertical lines for depression/anger. Red/dark auras.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Thin, precise dark brown/black lines.
Details: Very fine internal lines for muscles and face contours.
Hair Lines: Extremely delicate.
Material Variation: Flesh lines are softer/colored; clothing lines are crisper.

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Pale to fair (vampiric elegance often seen).
Shading Method: Soft airbrush gradients mixed with defined cell-shaded shadows under chin/hair.
Shadow Type: Often colored (purple/red) rather than grey.
Highlight Placement: Nose tip, lower lip, cheekbones, collarbones.

DISTINGUISHING STYLE MARKERS:
Intense, "predatory" or "obsessive" male gaze. Sharpness of features contrasting with soft coloring.

## === FEMALE CHARACTER DRAWING STYLE ===

PROPORTIONS SYSTEM:
Height Ratio: 7-8 heads tall.
Build Range: Slender, delicate but with realistic curves.
Structure: Narrow shoulders, defined waist, soft hips. Graceful neck.
Hands/Feet: Slender, elegant fingers, manicured nails.

FACIAL CONSTRUCTION:
Shape System: Soft oval/heart shape. Softer jawline than males but still defined (not round/blobby).
Eyes: Very large, dominant feature. "Jewel" eyes.
  - Construction: Rounder, wider aperture.
  - Detail Level: Galaxy-like irises with sparkles, multiple colors, and intricate pupil shapes.
  - Lashes/Brows: Thick, distinct individual eyelashes (volume). Thin, arched eyebrows.
Nose: Small, delicate button or straight nose. Often minimalized to a dot/shadow in front view.
Mouth: Small, rosebud lips. Glossy rendering.
Ears: Delicate, often hidden by hair or jewelry.

HAIR RENDERING:
Volume Approach: High volume (princess hair), defies gravity slightly.
Texture System: Soft, fluffy, billowy.
Strand Method: Large masses subdivided into fine flowing strands.
Line Style: Delicate, flowing.
Highlight Method: Soft diffuse glow + sparkles.
Physics: Always seemingly underwater or in a breeze (dramatic flow).

AGE REPRESENTATION: Young adult (late teens/20s). Smooth, flawless skin.

EXPRESSION MECHANICS:
Subtle Range: Blushing, averting gaze, biting lip.
Exaggerated Range: Tears (large, jewel-like), shock (pale face). Limited ugly crying.
Eye Changes: Shimmer/wobble for sadness. Flash/sparkle for joy.
Mouth Changes: Gentle smiles, O-shape surprise.
Special Effects: Sparkles, flower backgrounds, rosy cheeks.

LINE ART PHILOSOPHY:
Outline: Minimal, often colored (brown/pink) to blend with skin.
Details: Very few internal face lines to maintain "porcelain" look.
Hair Lines: Flowing, decorative.
Material Variation: Fabric lines vary (stiff for corsets, flowing for dresses).

COLOR & SHADING SYSTEM:
Skin Tone Range: Porcelain/Fair with pink undertones.
Shading Method: Softest gradients. Almost no hard shadows on face.
Shadow Type: Warm pinks/peaches.
Highlight Placement: High gloss on lips, nose, eyes, shoulders.

## === UNIVERSAL STYLE NOTES ===

GENDER DIMORPHISM LEVEL: High. Males are sharp/angular/intense; Females are soft/curved/ethereal.

CONSISTENCY RULES:
- Eyes are always the highest detail point.
- Hands are always realistic (no cartoon hands).
- Lighting is always dramatic/atmospheric.

VARIATION ALLOWANCES:
- Villains can look "uglier" (more lines, distorted expressions).
- Comedy beats can simplify faces slightly but rarely go full chibi.

AGE RANGE: Mostly young adults. Elderly are drawn with realistic wrinkles but dignity.

EXPRESSION SPECTRUM: Romantic Drama (High emotional intensity, swooning, obsession).

OVERALL AESTHETIC: High-End Korean Romance Fantasy (Rofan).
